EMPAYAR TANI supplies agricultural products and services located in Pontian, Johor. They have started their business since 2014 and are focusing mainly on supply of farm produces and farm supplies.​​​​​​​

They requested to have a website, with custom CMS (not using Wordpress, Joomla, etc...). So I created the custom CMS based on their requirements. The CMS allows the admin to create/edit Post Entry, the entry will be available on the front site once published. The admin can also set the template of the page, upload files and images, customize menu and setup slider.

I use Bootstrap for the skeleton of the styling, then I made a styling extension. The front page design is simple, section-by-section layout. The CMS design is influenced by Material Design, with floating buttons, collapsible menus, raised-up elements, lots of empty spaces.

The engine used is Xuxo PHP MVC with MySQL database.

The website is still up and running at http://www.empayartani.com.my/
