Saujana Gemilang Travel and Tour is a travel and tour company located in Gombak, Selangor. They promote affordable Umrah Tour.​​​​​​​

They requested to have a website to contain a booking system for their propective customers to book a tour package online.

The CSS is fully customized. The front page design is non-scrollable, highlighting the packages they offer, the ambassador and the CEO of their company.

The engine used is Xuxo PHP MVC with MySQL database.

Unfortunately, the website is already dead and discontinued.
