IUKL LMS is the portal for Infrastructure University Kuala Lumpur students, staffs and lecturers. Students can register their subjects for the semester, check their attendance, assigments, exams, notes, etc. Lecturers can post notes, assigments and manage their classes. Staffs can print report, fetch data and summaries.

I cannot show the content since it is confidential. I created the portal when I was working at IUKL, now I have transferred the responsibilities to good hands.
